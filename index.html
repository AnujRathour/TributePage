Captain Manoj Kumar Pandey

An Indian Army Officer who Warned the Death

Here is the time line of Capt.Pandey's life:

  • 1975-Born in Sitapur Uttar Pradesh,India
  • Education-Uttar Pradesh Sainik School, Lucknow
  • Graduation- National Defence Academy in 90th course and belonged to Mike Squadron (Mustangs)
  • 1997-Joined the army as lieutenant in the 1st battalion, 1/11 Gorkha Rifles
  • Wars-Kargil War/ Operation Vijay/ Battle of Kukarthan/ Battle of Khalubar
  • Gallantry award- Param Vir Chakra
  • Honours and legacy-A roundabout is also named after Martyr Capt.Manoj Pandey as "Captain Manoj Pandey Chowk" in his home district Sitapur, Uttar Pradesh

"If death strikes before I prove my blood, I swear, I will kill death!"

--Captain Manoj Kumar Pandey
